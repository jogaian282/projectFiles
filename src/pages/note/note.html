Cockroft's formula for calculating creatinine clearance

(140-Age) x Body wt (kg) x 0.85 if female ( gender factor 1:00 ± men /72 x serum creatinine mg/dl

For obese patients, use corrected body wt , BW = 1/3 extra wt

Normal values for creatinine clearence

75-115 in women

95-145 in men

Better to use EPI Formula (Age group above 50 years for calculated GFR
